Kanan: Simplifying Health Management for Seniors

Kanan is a mobile app that helps older adults take better care of their health. It's like a friendly assistant on their phones that makes it easier to remember when to take medicine, keep track of doctor's appointments, and see important health information. Features

Health Information

The Health Information feature in the Kanan app allows users to view important health information about themselves, such as chronic illnesses or surgeries. This feature provides a simple and easy-to-use interface for users to access their health information, which can help seniors to manage their health needs more effectively. The Doctor's Contacts feature in the Kanan app allows users to keep track of their doctors and their contact information.
